About Doornox

Doornox helps you connect to job posters directly

Tired of trying to get a recruiter's attention by posting your resume on a job board? You don't need to apply to black holes to get your foot in the door anymore. 40% of all hires come through employee referrals. Doornox helps you find employees who can refer you.

Doornox is unique in that job posters post jobs for free to earn company referral bonuses and to help their companies hire. You, the job seeker, search their job posts and message them privately for referrals. This means more opportunities are posted for you, and you can directly contact employees on the inside. Doornox is simply a network to find and give referrals.


Why the name Doornox?

"When one door closes, another opens," and in the metaphorical sense, that's what our name means. Nox's Latin roots mean "night," or "to close," but spoken out loud, Nox is to knock and therefore to open doors. Doornox is symbolic for both closing and opening the right doors to help you find fulfilling work.
